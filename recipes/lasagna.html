Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish made by layering flat lasagna noodles with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan. The layers are assembled and baked until the cheese is melted and bubbly, creating a hearty, comforting meal. Its flavors are a perfect blend of savory tomato sauce, seasoned meat, and creamy cheeses, making it a popular family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef or sausage
  • 24 oz (680 g) jar of marinara sauce
  • 2 cups (450 g) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups (200 g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup (50 g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the Noodles:
    • Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the Meat:
    • In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef or sausage until browned. Drain excess fat.
    • Add the marinara sauce to the cooked meat and stir to combine. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Make the Cheese Mixture:
    • In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, 1 cup of mozzarella, and 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a 9x13 inch (23x33 cm) baking dish.
    • Add a layer of lasagna noodles (about 3 noodles.)
    • Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    • Add 1/3 of the meat sauce on top.
    • Repeat layers (noodles, ricotta, sauce) two more times, finishing with a layer of sauce.
  5. Top with Cheese and Bake:
    • Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
    • Cover the dish with aluminum foil (to prevent over-browning) and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Let It Rest:
    • Remove from the oven and let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.
